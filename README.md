## 👋 About Me
I'm a senior Cybersecurity student at Southern Utah University (graduating May 2026) with a minor in Computer Science and a 3.6 major GPA. Growing up as a military child instilled in me a deep sense of responsibility, precision, and a drive to protect the people and systems that others depend on.
I have hands-on experience in enterprise IT infrastructure, network configuration, and system administration through my internship at Marshall Industries, where I deployed and configured 50+ enterprise workstation systems and troubleshot Cisco network infrastructure. Outside of class and work, I'm constantly building and experimenting in my own home lab because I believe the best security professionals never stop learning on their own time.
My long-term goal is to bridge the gap between technical teams and the people they serve. Whether that means stepping into a security leadership role or eventually owning my own business. I'm particularly passionate about offensive security, red teaming, and understanding how attackers think so I can build better defenses.
